Bio

I believe that engineering biology as a computational and metabolic machine constitutes the future of bioscience, and that synthetic biology will revolutionize how we approach our most pressing scientific pursuits, both here on earth and among the stars.

Wetware

  • Optogenetic & chemical manipulation of bacterial gene expression using custom hardware, both in liquid culture and in conjunction with microscopy
  • Bacterial gene circuits, plasmid design & molecular cloning of DNA

Software

  • Proficient with Python. Created a custom package, PyHMMER, that enables high-throughput analysis of all NCBI genomes to mine co-localized proteins matching arbitrary hidden Markov models (using HMMER).
  • Comfortable with JavaScript and AngularJS. Contributed substantially to development of a webapp used to program and simulate dynamic optogenetic time courses for use with our custom hardware. ( Iris)
  • Comfortable with version control (Git, GitHub)
  • Familiar with C, Java, LaTeX, SolidWorks, Inkscape, Linux

Hardware

  • Comfortable with basic circuit design, PCB design & routing, surface-mount soldering, assembly, and debugging.
  • Comfortable with basic 3D design in SolidWorks and 3D printing (PLA extrusion)
  • Experience with sub-milliliter fluidic devices and metered flow systems
